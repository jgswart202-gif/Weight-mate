1. Overview

Weight Tracker is designed to help you log body weight, visualize progress, and set goals. We take privacy seriously and aim to collect no personal data beyond what is necessary to provide core functionality. This policy explains what data the App handles, how it is used, and your choices.

The App is primarily offline. Your entries are stored locally on your device via an on‑device database (SQLite). No cloud sync is performed by default.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not require an account and does not transmit data to our servers. Data handled by the App includes:

  • Weight entries: numeric values you input, stored locally with date/time and optional notes.
  • Settings: goal weight, unit preferences, and reminder preferences (e.g., notifications on/off).

No names, email addresses, location, contacts, photos, or other personally identifiable information are collected by default.

3. App Permissions

The App may request the following permission(s) to enable specific features:

  • Notifications (Android: POST_NOTIFICATIONS on Android 13+): used to send daily reminders to log weight.

You can disable notifications anytime in the App (if provided) or your device settings. The App does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

4. How We Use Information

  • Display your weight history and progress charts on your device.
  • Calculate progress toward your goal weight.
  • Send local reminder notifications (no server involved).

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Your data is stored locally on your device in an on‑device database (SQLite).
  • We do not retain copies of your data on our servers.
  • Uninstalling the App will remove locally stored data. We recommend exporting/backing up data (if such a feature is provided) before uninstalling.

6. Sharing & Third‑Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal data. The App does not integrate analytics or advertising SDKs by default. If optional third‑party services are added in the future (e.g., cloud sync), we will update this policy and present clear consent options.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data stored on your device. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. Use device‑level protections such as a screen lock to help safeguard your data.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us so we can assist with removal instructions.

9. Your Rights

Because data is stored locally, you can modify or delete entries directly within the App. If export or backup features are provided, you can use them to obtain a copy of your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be highlighted in‑app where appropriate.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@example.com.
